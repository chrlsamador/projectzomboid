About This Game

Project Zomboid is an open-ended zombie-infested sandbox. It asks one simple question – how will you die?

the towns of Muldraugh and West Point, survivors must loot houses, build defences and do their utmost to delay their inevitable death day by day. No help is coming – their continued survival relies on their own cunning, luck and ability to evade a relentless horde.

  • Hardcore Sandbox Zombie Survival Game with a focus on realistic survival.
  • Online multiplayer survival with persistent player-run servers.
  • Local 4 player split-screen co-op.
  • Hundreds of zombies with swarm mechanics and in-depth visual and hearing systems.
  • Full line of sight system and real-time lighting, sound and visibility mechanics.
  • Vast and growing map (loosely based on a real world location) for you to explore.
  • Vehicles with full physics and deep and realistic gameplay mechanics.
  • Use tools and items to craft weapons, barricade and cook.
  • Deal with depression, boredom, hunger, thirst and illness while trying to survive.
  • Day turns to night. The electricity falters. Hordes migrate.
  • Farming, fishing, carpentry, cooking, trapping, character customization.
  • Proper zombies that don’t run (unless you change sandbox settings).
  • A ton of atmospheric music tracks by Zach Beever.
  • Challenge scenarios and instant action ‘Last Stand’ mode.
  • Full, open and powerful Lua modding support.
  • Xbox Controller Gamepad support on Windows.
